How to REALLY clear cache:

  1. Press Ctrl + Shift + Delete
  2. Select "Cached images and files"
  3. Select "All time"
  4. Click "Clear data"
  5. Close ALL browser tabs
  6. Reopen browser and go to localhost:3000
